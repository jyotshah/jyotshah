WorkNest – Crew & Task Manager

WorkNest is a mobile application designed for restaurant owners and small business managers to easily organize and oversee their operations. Built using Kotlin and SQLite for robust local data management, the app allows users to handle crew scheduling, task tracking, and expense management—all in one place.

Users can add and edit team members with role details and availability, create and prioritize tasks, manage business expenses with downloadable reports, and receive smart notifications for important updates. WorkNest also leverages Android system services like broadcast receivers to respond to battery status, internet changes, and device reboots, ensuring the app remains reliable in any situation.

With its clean dashboard, offline functionality, and lightweight performance, WorkNest simplifies the daily hustle of running a business and empowers managers to stay in control effortlessly.
